Privacy Policy

This privacy policy applies to the mobile application "Ground To Air Slicer" published by Motoki Furusawa. On the Google Play Store, this application is published under the developer name "UPLINK_GAMER", which is operated by the same individual.

Information Collection
This app does not collect, store, or use any personally identifiable information from users. However, the app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements, which may collect certain device-related and usage data.

Third-Party Services
The app integrates Google AdMob, which may collect the following types of information:

  • Device information
  • App usage data
  • Location data
This data is used to provide relevant ads and improve the user experience. Data collected by AdMob is handled according to Google’s Privacy Policy and Google’s Advertising Policies.

Users can control ad personalization in their device settings.

Data Storage
This app stores user-configured settings and gameplay data only on the user’s device. These data are not transmitted externally.

Provision to Third Parties
Other than Google AdMob as mentioned above, this app does not share user information with third parties.

Changes
This privacy policy may change without notice. Updates will be reflected within this document.
